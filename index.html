NovaHacks

NovaHacks is a 24 hour hackathon (coding competition) for highschool students. Over the February 22-23 weekend, over 100 students will come to the Northern Virginia area to compete for bragging rights and awesome prizes.

FAQ

Who can come?

Any high school student in grades 9 through 12 can come to the event. You don't need to live in Virginia, either. Anyone in the United States can attend. We hope you can make it!

Do I need to know how to code?

No! You don't have to have any prior coding knowledge, although it helps. If you want to come just to learn and not to compete, thats fine.

How big can teams be?

The maximum team size is 4. Also, you can form teams prior to the event. Signup with you friends and work together on a project (as long as you start coding at the event.)

Does it cost anything?

Nope! This hackathon is 100% free for all attendees. The cost is covered by our wonderful sponsors, who are listed below. Also, food and snacks will be provided at the event.

Apply

Unfortunately, we do have a maximum capacity for participants. As such, it is best to apply early so as not to risk losing a spot at the hackathon when applications close. To apply, you need to provide name, age, grade, and sign liability forms as well as other legal forms our venue requires.

To get started with this process, click the button in the top right corner.
