Make

NovaHacks is a 24 hour hackathon (coding competition) for highschool students. Over the February 22-23 weekend, over 100 students will come to the Northern Virginia area to compete for bragging rights and awesome prizes.

FAQ

Who can come?

Any high school student in grades 9 through 12 can come to the event. You don't need to live in Virginia, either. Anyone in the United States can attend. We hope you can make it!

Is this event supervised?

Although this event was planned and organized by high school students, there are adults and mentors at the event. In short, it is fully supervised by 20+ adults.

I'm new to coding...

That's ok! You don't have to have any prior coding knowledge, although it helps. If you want to come just to learn and not to compete, thats fine.

Where will I sleep?

We will provide quiet places to sleep at the event, and turn lights off after the midnight snack. We’ll make sure you’re able to get some sleep if you need it!

How big can teams be?

The maximum team size is 4. Also, you can form teams prior to the event. Signup with your friends and work together on a project (as long as you start coding at the event.)

Do I need a team?

Nope. You can work solo or with friends. You can even arrive at the event without anyone and look for teammates. Or, you can join our slack above and ask around if anyone is looking for a team.

Does it cost anything?

Nope! This hackathon is 100% free for all attendees. The cost is covered by our wonderful sponsors, who are listed below. Also, food and snacks will be provided at the event.

What should I bring?

You should bring a computer, chargers, any hardware you think you will need, toiletries, & a sleeping bag. Additionally, bring a student ID for check in.

Apply

Unfortunately, we do have a maximum capacity for participants. As such, it is best to apply early so as not to risk losing a spot at the hackathon when applications close. To apply, you need to provide name, age, grade, and sign liability forms as well as other legal forms our venue requires.

To get started with this process, click the button in the top right corner.
